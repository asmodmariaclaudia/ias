About

Hello! My name is Maria Claudia A. Asmod, and I'm a 20-year-old BSIT student currently in my third year at Gingoog City Colleges, Inc. While I’m still building my knowledge in cybersecurity, I’m deeply passionate about learning and excited to explore the ever-evolving world of technology.

I created this website as a final output for my subject, Information Assurance and Security 2 (IAS 102). Through this project, I aim to gain a deeper understanding of the threats associated with the Internet of Things (IoT)—a rapidly growing area of concern in today's highly connected digital landscape. As more everyday devices become linked to the internet, the risks to privacy, data security, and system integrity continue to rise. By exploring this topic, I hope to shed light on the vulnerabilities within IoT systems and contribute to the conversation on how we can better protect users and infrastructure from emerging cyber threats.

Originally from Purok 4 Tambagan Medina, Misamis Oriental. A graduate from Medina National High School. A Transferee from University of Science and Technology of Southern Philippines (USTP) Cagayan de Oro City, where I took up Bachelor of Science in Information Technology (BSIT).

Even as a student, I am motivated to take part in the ongoing conversation about cybersecurity and its far-reaching effects on society. I believe that by sharing knowledge, encouraging awareness, and engaging others in meaningful discussions, I can make a modest yet meaningful contribution to promoting a more informed and resilient digital community.

Thank you for taking the time to visit my website. I hope the information presented here provides you with valuable insights and encourages deeper reflection on the challenges of cybersecurity. Together, let us navigate the complexities of this ever-changing field and strive to build a safer, more secure digital future for all.
